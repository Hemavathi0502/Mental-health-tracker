Top 5 Power Nap Tips

1. *Keep it Short* – Aim for 10–20 minutes to avoid grogginess.
2. *Find a Quiet Spot* – Reduce light and noise to fall asleep faster.
3. *Set an Alarm* – Avoid oversleeping by using a timer or app.
4. *Nap Early in the Day* – Napping too late can disrupt nighttime sleep.
5. *Create a Routine* – Regular napping can train your body to rest effectively.
